How GratiTree Works

We believe that daily positive reflection leads to a happier mindset and better mental health.

1

Write an Entry

Each day, write a new entry for something your grateful for. It can be as simple or complex as you like.

2

Add a Leaf

Your daily entries become leaves that get placed onto your own GratiTree.

3

Watch Your Tree Grow

As you make entries throughout the year, your GratiTree will grow bigger and bigger. At the start of every year you can start a new tree!
