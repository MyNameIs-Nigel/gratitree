Our mission

We're GratiTree‚Äîan organization that wants to make gratitude visible and shareable. We believe positive reflection is more powerful when it becomes a collective ritual: a public tree that grows from what people appreciate every day.

1

Add up to 3 gratitudes

Each day, write up to three short entries that you're grateful for. Keep it simple‚Äîone line is enough.

2

Branches form from similar words

When multiple people mention related ideas, the tree ‚Äúthickens‚Äù that branch‚Äîrevealing shared themes.

3

Your message becomes a leaf

Every entry becomes a leaf you (and others) can like and comment on while the tree is open.

Community interaction

You can like and comment on leaves to encourage others. Once midnight hits, the day's tree locks‚Äîpreserving it as a snapshot of that day's gratitude.

  • Tree is open during the day for new leaves, likes, and comments
  • At midnight, it locks and a new tree starts
  • Previous trees remain visible for reflection

7-day window

You can revisit and interact with trees up to 7 days old (like/comment). This keeps the space present-focused, while still allowing meaningful follow-ups.

  • Browse daily trees in a timeline
  • Like/comment on trees up to 7 days old
  • Older trees remain readable (optional, based on your product decisions)
